Using the bulk downloader

The bulk downloader is a simple yet powerful tool that helps you download lyrics for entire playlists of tracks, or even your entire iTunes library.
To use it, open the bulk downloader window by pressing ⌘B or clicking Lyric > "Bulk downloader..."

Once open, simply select a playlist in the list to the left and click "Go" to start the download. Progress will be displayed while the bulk downloader is working.
When the bulk downloader is finished processing a track, an icon representing either success or failure will be displayed.
